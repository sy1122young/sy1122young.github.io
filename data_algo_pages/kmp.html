KMP data compression

Description

The KMP (Knuth-Morris-Pratt) Data Compression Algorithm is a novel approach to data compression, leveraging the principles of pattern matching and efficient string searching. Inspired by the KMP algorithm's ability to detect repeating patterns without redundant computations, this compression technique optimally identifies and encodes repetitive sequences in data, reducing storage requirements while maintaining data integrity. This algorithm is particularly effective for datasets with recurring substrings, offering a blend of speed and high compression ratios.
